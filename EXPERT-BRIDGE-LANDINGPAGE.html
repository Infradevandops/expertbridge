Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure quality if engineers are based in Nigeria/India?

Every engineer undergoes the same 4-stage audit regardless of location. Our 85/100 minimum score and 3% acceptance rate ensure only elite talent joins the bench. We also conduct quarterly re-audits to maintain standards.

What if the engineer doesn't work out?

We offer a 2-week satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll replace the engineer at no additional cost or provide a full refund.

How quickly can you deploy talent?

Our average time-to-placement is 48 hours for standard roles (Full-Stack, Cloud). Specialized roles (e.g., AI/ML, Blockchain) may take 3-5 days.

What are your rates?

Staff augmentation rates range from $90-$120/hour depending on specialization and seniority. Managed projects are quoted on a fixed-fee basis. This represents 40-60% cost savings compared to US-based contractors.

What timezone do your engineers work in?

Most engineers are based in GMT+1 (Nigeria) or GMT+5:30 (India), providing 4-6 hour overlap with US Eastern time. We can also accommodate full US timezone coverage for premium engagements.

Do you offer FTE conversion?

Yes. After 6 months of engagement, clients can convert contractors to full-time employees with a one-time placement fee (typically 15-20% of annual salary).
